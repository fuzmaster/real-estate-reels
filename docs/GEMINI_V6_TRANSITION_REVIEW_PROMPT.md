# Gemini V6 Transition Review Prompt

Copy this into Gemini with the newest MP4 attached.

```txt
You are a ruthless senior real estate video editor, motion graphics director, SaaS product strategist, and Remotion reviewer.

I am building Real Estate Reels, a web app that lets real estate agents upload listing photos, frame each photo, choose a video style, choose transition presets, add branding, and render short-form MP4 listing videos.

This is V6.

The newest change is a Photo Transition Style system. The app now supports transition choices such as Smart Mix, Soft Fade, Slide In, Slide Up, Zoom Pop, Whip Pan, Flash Cut, and No Transition.

The goal is not to build a timeline editor. The goal is to give agents simple preset controls that make the final reel feel professionally edited.

Review the attached render brutally.

Do not be polite.

Assume the viewer is scrolling Instagram or TikTok and decides in under one second whether to keep watching.

Assume the buyer is a real estate agent who may pay $29/month only if this makes their listings look more professional than their competition.

First, give me a brutal verdict.

Then score these from 1 to 10:
- First frame
- Photo order
- Photo framing
- Motion polish
- Transition choice
- Pacing
- Music fit
- Music-to-edit relationship
- Text readability
- Safe zones
- Agent branding
- CTA strength
- Template uniqueness
- Would an agent pay?

For each score, give:
1. Why it got that score
2. Exact fix
3. Must-fix before sales demo or later improvement

Then evaluate the transition system:
- Which transition preset looks best?
- Which preset looks cheapest?
- Which should be free?
- Which should be Pro?
- Are transitions helping the reel feel edited, or distracting from the house?
- Should the app keep transition choices visible, or hide them under Advanced?
- Should the default stay Smart Mix?

Then tell me whether the reel still feels like:
A. A slideshow
B. A Canva export
C. A decent social ad
D. A professional edited reel

Then rank what to build next:
A. Beat-synced cuts
B. Built-in ElevenLabs music moods
C. Live Remotion preview player
D. More templates
E. Better typography/motion graphics
F. Accounts/database/payments

Finally answer clearly:
Is this ready to show a real estate agent as a sales demo?
YES or NO.

If NO, list the minimum changes needed before showing it.
If YES, tell me what kind of agent would buy first and what price I should test.
```
