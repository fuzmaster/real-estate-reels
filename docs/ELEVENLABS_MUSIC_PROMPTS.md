# ElevenLabs Music Mood Prompts

Use these to generate a starter library later.

## 1. Luxury Cinematic
Mood: elegant, premium, calm confidence.
Genre: cinematic ambient.
Tempo: 70 BPM.
Instruments: soft piano, warm cello, atmospheric pads, subtle percussion.
Use case: luxury listings, twilight exteriors, high-end homes.
Avoid: vocals, trap hats, aggressive bass, sudden drops.

## 2. Upbeat Open House
Mood: bright, welcoming, energetic.
Genre: pop instrumental.
Tempo: 118 BPM.
Instruments: bright piano, claps, clean drums, light synth bass.
Use case: open house reels and standard family homes.
Avoid: vocals, dark chords, heavy EDM drops.

## 3. Modern Lo-Fi Relax
Mood: smooth, modern, relaxed.
Genre: lo-fi hip-hop instrumental.
Tempo: 85 BPM.
Instruments: soft drums, Rhodes keys, vinyl texture, warm bass.
Use case: condos, apartments, cozy interiors.
Avoid: dirty distortion, aggressive drums, distracting melodies.

## 4. High Energy Social
Mood: fast, punchy, thumb-stopping.
Genre: future bass / social media pop.
Tempo: 125 BPM.
Instruments: punchy kick, sidechained synths, crisp snares, subtle vocal chops.
Use case: short 10-15 second TikTok/Reels edits.
Avoid: long intros, vocals with lyrics, muddy low end.

## 5. Corporate Professional
Mood: clean, safe, polished.
Genre: corporate tech instrumental.
Tempo: 108 BPM.
Instruments: muted guitar, light shaker, soft synth, clean drums.
Use case: brokerages, commercial real estate, conservative brands.
Avoid: trendy trap sounds, comedy tones, heavy bass.

## 6. Warm & Inviting
Mood: friendly, homey, optimistic.
Genre: acoustic pop instrumental.
Tempo: 100 BPM.
Instruments: acoustic guitar, upright bass, soft percussion, light piano.
Use case: starter homes and suburban family homes.
Avoid: cheesy ukulele overload, vocals, children sounds.

## 7. Urgent Price Drop
Mood: urgent, energetic, action-oriented.
Genre: synthwave / electronic.
Tempo: 128 BPM.
Instruments: arpeggiated synths, driving kick, tight snare, pulsing bass.
Use case: price drop, back on market, limited-time reels.
Avoid: horror tones, panic sounds, harsh distortion.

## 8. Sophisticated Minimalist
Mood: sleek, modern, quiet luxury.
Genre: minimal electronic.
Tempo: 90 BPM.
Instruments: plucks, deep soft bass, subtle percussion, airy pads.
Use case: modern builds, new construction, empty staged homes.
Avoid: busy melodies, acoustic folk elements, vocals.
