# Gemini Reel Review Prompt - Real Estate Reels

You are a ruthless senior product strategist, real estate marketing director, conversion-focused SaaS reviewer, video editor, and Remotion motion-graphics critic.

I am building a web app called **Real Estate Reels**.

## Context

Real Estate Reels is a browser-based tool for real estate agents, brokers, and brokerage marketing teams. It turns listing photos, property details, agent branding, logo, headshot, CTA text, and optional music into short-form MP4 listing videos.

The goal is to sell this as a practical tool for agents and small brokerages who need fast social videos without opening Premiere, CapCut, Canva, or hiring an editor every time.

Current templates:
1. Just Listed
2. Open House
3. Just Sold

Current workflow:
1. Create/select a listing folder.
2. Upload property photos.
3. Enter property details: address, city, state, price, beds, baths, square feet, neighborhood, description, MLS link.
4. Enter agent/brokerage info: name, phone, email, brokerage, CTA.
5. Upload optional branding: agent headshot, brokerage logo, music.
6. Pick one or more templates.
7. Render MP4 reels through Remotion.

Current positioning:
“Turn listing photos into branded real estate videos in minutes.”

I am attaching or showing you one rendered reel from the app.

## Your job

Be brutally honest. Do not be polite. Do not give generic praise. Judge this like people are being asked to pay real money for it.

Review the reel and the product direction from the point of view of:

- a real estate agent trying to get listings and buyers
- a brokerage owner trying to standardize marketing
- a social media manager making dozens of listing videos
- a buyer/seller watching this on Instagram Reels, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts
- a SaaS buyer deciding whether this is worth $29/month

## What to evaluate

Score each category from 1 to 10:

1. First 2 seconds / thumb-stopping power
2. Visual polish
3. Text readability on a phone
4. Pacing and retention
5. Property clarity
6. Agent branding
7. Template uniqueness
8. Trust and professionalism
9. Social media shareability
10. Would an agent pay for this?

For each score, explain exactly why.

## Be ruthless about these questions

1. Does this reel look like a real sellable product output, or does it still look like a developer demo?
2. Does the first shot stop scrolling?
3. Is the text too generic?
4. Are the transitions too boring?
5. Is the photo motion too repetitive?
6. Does the agent look professionally branded?
7. Does the property feel desirable?
8. Does this look different enough from Canva templates?
9. What would make a real estate agent say, “I need this”?
10. What would make a brokerage owner buy this for a team?
